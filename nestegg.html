What is NestEgg?

At NestEgg, our aim was to empower ordinary people to achieve financial independence through real-estate ownership. We did this by providing an ecosystem that allows mom and pop landlords to easily self-manage their own portfolios without hiring a property manager. NestEgg was acquired by Truehold in 2022.
