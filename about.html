ABOUT ME

PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUND

My journey started in high school, when I designed custom t-shirts on my friend's screen-printing machine and toted around a moleskine notebook, writing the 'Next Great American Novel'. Since then, I've learned the ins-and-outs of computer science, honed my graphic design skills in a variety of gigs, worked as a content-writer, and combined all of these skills to design and build mobile products and web applications.

Currently, I'm on the search for my next opportunity. Ideally, I'm hoping to help shape the future of human-technology interactions through design initiatives that improve people's lives.

Outside of work

Outside of work, I'm passionate about investing in the next generation, which I do through mentoring and coaching college students. This past year I led an addiction-recovery group for students struggling with internet-related addictions. A formative experience for me was volunteering at a refugee camp in Greece in 2017.

GET CONNECTED
