About This Site

Built by the Asian at Hinge ERG

Every May, Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage Month gives us a chance to celebrate the richness and diversity of AAPI cultures — through food, art, literature, and gathering. This site was made with love by Hinge's AAPI Employee Resource Group to share our community's favorite spots, events, reads, and traditions with the broader Hinge team.

Want to add a restaurant, event, or book recommendation? Reach out to the Asian at Hinge ERG on Slack.
