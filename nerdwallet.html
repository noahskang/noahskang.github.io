Context

NerdWallet’s mission is to empower users with the information they need to make life’s financial decisions with confidence. The rise of LLMs presented both a challenge and an opportunity to advance this mission. I led a hackathon team to create an MVP of a chat module incorporating ChatGPT technology, winning the top award and inspiring leadership to establish a dedicated AI team, where I led design.

Objectives

  • User Needs: Provide answers to complex financial questions quickly and accurately.
  • Business Needs: Differentiate NerdWallet from LLM competitors with a unique, trustworthy chat experience.
  • Hypothesis: Integrating a chat experience that answers user queries and directs them to fact-checked NerdWallet content will create a uniquely reliable service.

User Research

We conducted extensive user research, including surveys, preference tests, and video-recorded prototype tests with early design iterations. I designed and executed user research our research studies, collaborating with product to design research that accurately pinpointed user pain points and needs.

Key Insights

  • Usefulness: Users struggle with information overload from lengthy articles. They value time and efficiency, making an AI chat that provides quick, precise answers highly desirable.
  • Trustworthiness: Users are skeptical of LLMs. Linking responses to NerdWallet’s expert-written content helps build trust.
  • Usefulness: Amidst NerdWallet's vast content, the chat needs to stand out without overwhelming the user interface.
  • Discoverability: Users struggle with information overload from lengthy articles. They value time and efficiency, making an AI chat that provides quick, precise answers highly desirable.
  • Clarity: Users often confuse AI chats with traditional chatbots. Clearly signaling the advanced capabilities of the chat is crucial.

Synthesis

Collaborating closely with product, engineering, and design teams, I synthesized research findings into actionable insights, sharing these through detailed research decks. This process ensured cross-functional alignment and buy-in.

Requirements

I partnered with engineering and product teams to define project requirements, milestones, and pathways, ensuring efficient time management and resource allocation. Regular syncs with key stakeholders kept expectations realistic and aligned.

Handoff

To ensure seamless translation of design to dev, I thoroughly annotate designs, write up design requirements, and conduct walk-throughs for the engineers. This is more efficient than trying to correct errors after development, when code is already in stage.

Post-Development

Analysis

Post-development, I used user research studies to qualitatively assess the success of our designs. This supplemented the quantitative data we used to measure engagement. It’s critical to employ user research at the end of the design process as well as at the beginning in order to test hypotheses and get a more tactile sense of how to improve for the future.
